Welcome to Cross Brothers Services LLC

We are a local business...

Cross Brothers Services LLC is a local family-owned company specialized in offering exceptional and captivating landscaping services. Even though we are located in Lewis County, we do serve the majority of our clients throughout Thurston County and other areas of Western Washington.

We start by listening to you...

The first thing we do is listen to what you want. Going for an all new look? Do you have kids and/or pets who will be enjoying this space? Do you love gardening or not so much? Listening to your ideas as the homeowner is how we make your house a home.

We work within your budget.

Each landscape is as unique as its client, and so are budgets. We work hard to provide our clients with the most accurate prices to deliver results that make you feel good about your yard.

Residential/Commercial Landscape Maintenance and Lawn Care

Landscape and lawn maintenance is a service where thorough care is given to keep the lawn green, beautiful, weed-free, and healthy. When lawn care is neglected, it starts patching, weeding, and drying. Scheduling regular maintenance with our professional landscapers will keep your lawn healthy and attractive.

Clean Ups

Spring is a significant time to clean up your lawn after the long winter months. Fall is the time to make your lawn prepare for the harsh winter months ahead. Thus, fall and spring clean-ups are crucial for lawn owners to keep it beautiful and hygienic.
